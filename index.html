Lytyfy is a financial technology company offering flexible financing options to the unbanked and underbanked population in India to help them access essential products and services. We’re on a mission to provide simpler and affordable ways to purchase and finance essential consumer products likes solar home systems, smartphones and laptops to the low and middle income customer segment in India.

Work

Lytyfy is currently focused on clean energy financing space and reimagining how clean energy products and services are financed and distributed to the underbanked market segment in India. We are building an integrated value chain that can provide good quality solar products and service to our customers right at their doorsteps with flexible repayment options.

Projects

Our solar financing platform lyte connects individual and institutional investors with curated solar energy projects and creditworthy borrowers willing to purchase solar off-grid products.
